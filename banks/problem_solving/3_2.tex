Jennifer bought a box of Crunchy Grain cereal.  
The nutrition facts on the box state that a serving size of the cereal is $\frac{3}{4}$ cup and provides 210 calories, 50 of which are calories from fat.  
In addition, each serving of the cereal provides 180 milligrams of potassium, which is 5\% of the daily allowance for adults.  
If $p$ percent of an adult’s daily allowance of potassium is provided by $x$ servings of Crunchy Grain cereal per day, which of the following expresses $p$ in terms of $x$?

\vspace{0.75em}

\choicebox{A}{$p = 0.5x$}  
\choicebox{B}{$p = 5x$}  
\choicebox{C}{$p = (0.05)^x$}  
\choicebox{D}{$p = (1.05)^x$}
