1 · Getting started 开始使用

  1. Get your account from {{ site_teacher_label }} Your teacher creates a personal username and password. You cannot self-register. 向老师领取专属账号和密码，网站不支持自行注册。
  2. Open the site and sign in Go to Sign in and enter your username and password. 打开网站，点击 Sign in，输入账号密码登录。
  3. Start with Workspace After login, click Workspace in the top menu to open SAT Math practice. 登录后点击顶部 Workspace 进入 SAT 数学练习区。

2 · How to practice 如何练习

Units 1–4

Specialized training

Four full SAT Math units: Algebra, Advanced Math, Problem Solving & Data, Geometry. Start with a full unit or a chapter slice (e.g. 1.1–1.5).

四个完整单元：代数、高等数学、数据分析、几何。可按整单元或分章节练习。

Hard drill

Sets I–XIV

Fourteen advanced problem sets for students aiming at top scores. Each set has a scored session and report.

14 套进阶难题，适合冲刺高分。每套做完有分数和报告。

After each set

Session report

When you finish (or pause), open the session summary to see score, accuracy, and each question reviewed.

完成或暂停后，打开 Session Report 查看得分和逐题解析。

Review loop

Mistake analytics

Every wrong answer is saved. Tag mistakes, add notes, and use redo links until you master the topic.

错题自动保存，可打标签、写笔记、反复重做直到掌握。

3 · For parents 给家长

  • One account per student — progress, scores, and mistakes stay tied to that login.
    每位学生独立账号，进度与成绩单独保存。
  • Check progress at home — sign in with your child’s account to see unit progress on the Dashboard and recent session scores.
    可用孩子账号登录，在 Dashboard 查看单元进度和最近练习分数。
  • Mistake analytics — see which topics need work and whether your child has redone missed questions.
    在 Mistake analytics 查看薄弱知识点和错题重做情况。
  • Need an account? — contact {{ site_teacher_label }} or {{ site_support_contact }}.
    需要账号请联系 {{ site_teacher_label }}。

4 · Tips 小提示

  • Use a laptop or tablet for the best experience; the scratchpad works on touch screens.
    建议用电脑或平板；触摸屏可使用草稿板。
  • Complete at least a short set (3+ questions) so progress counts on your Dashboard.
    每次至少完成一小套（3 题以上），Dashboard 才会记录进度。
  • “Exam mode” and AP / ISEE tracks are coming soon — stick to Units 1–4 and Hard Drill for now.
    模考和 AP/ISEE 尚未上线，目前请使用 Units 1–4 和 Hard Drill。
  • Forgot your password? Ask {{ site_teacher_label }} to reset it from Admin.
    忘记密码请联系老师重置。
